About Humble Bundle

Humble Bundle sells games, books, software, and other digital content. What makes Humble Bundle unique is its "pay what you want" pricing model and the ability to support charity with every purchase.

Since its launch in 2010, Humble Bundle has raised millions of dollars for various charities around the world. The bundles are available for a limited time, and new bundles are added regularly. Customers can choose how their payment is divided between the content creators, Humble Bundle, and the selected charities.

Key Features:

  • Pay what you want pricing
  • Support for various charities
  • High-quality games, books, and software
  • Regularly updated bundles
